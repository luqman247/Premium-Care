## Final image generation prompt

““Morning Light, Quiet Confidence”” — Primary homepage hero — the first image every visitor sees

A 78-year-old Danish woman with silver hair sits by a large north-facing window in her classic Copenhagen apartment. She wears a soft cashmere cardigan in warm beige. Morning light falls gently across her face. A PremiumCare professional in a natural linen uniform sits beside her, not looking at camera, both sharing a quiet moment. The apartment features mid-century modern furniture, oak floors, a single potted plant, and white walls. The mood is peaceful, dignified, and warm. Shot on medium format digital camera. Natural window light only. Shallow depth of field. Editorial photography style. No direct eye contact with camera. No exaggerated smiles. Scandinavian interior design. Film-like colour rendering with muted tones.

Environment: Classic Danish apartment, high ceilings, large windows, mid-century chair, oak floors, single healthy plant, minimal but personal objects

Emotional objective: To immediately communicate that PremiumCare is about dignity, calm, and human connection — not medical intervention

Mood: Peaceful contentment, quiet companionship, dignified calm

Composition: Two-shot, subjects positioned left of centre, large window creates negative space right, shallow depth of field (f/2.8), generous headroom

Lighting: Large north-facing window, overcast morning light, soft shadows on the right side of faces

Wardrobe: Client: cashmere cardigan (warm beige), wool trousers (charcoal). Care professional: natural linen uniform (soft grey), leather shoes

Camera: Hasselblad X2D, 16-bit colour, natural contrast, film-like rendering. 50mm equivalent (natural perspective)

Colour palette: #F5F3EF, #E8E4DE, #A8B5A0, #B4C4C8, #D4C4B0. Dominant tones: #F5F3EF, #E8E4DE, #A8B5A0.

landscape composition, 2200×1467px (1.50:1 aspect ratio).

Editorial photography for PremiumCare — a Danish home care institution. Scandinavian aesthetic: calm, dignified, human, never performative. Natural Nordic light, muted film-like colour rendering. No direct eye contact with camera unless the brief specifies otherwise. No exaggerated smiles, no clinical or hospital aesthetics. No visible medical equipment unless explicitly described. Subjects treated with dignity — never pitied. Photorealistic, medium-format digital camera quality
