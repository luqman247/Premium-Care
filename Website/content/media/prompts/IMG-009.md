““Physiotherapy — Gentle Movement”” — Illustrate physiotherapy service with grace and patience

A PremiumCare physiotherapist guides an elderly Danish man through a gentle standing exercise in a bright, minimalist room. The physiotherapist stands beside him, hands lightly supporting his posture, not holding him. The client stands tall, looking forward with quiet determination. The room has white walls, oak floors, a large window with natural light, and a single yoga mat in natural linen. No clinical equipment. The mood is calm, focused, and dignified. Shot on medium format digital. 50mm lens. Natural window light. Shallow depth of field. Editorial photography. Scandinavian minimalism. Muted, warm tones.
