““Team Culture — The Gathering”” — Illustrate team culture as supportive, human, and connected

Six Premium Care professionals in natural linen uniforms gather in a bright staff room, sitting on comfortable Danish design sofas and chairs in a circle. They hold ceramic mugs of coffee and tea. Some are talking, some listening, all engaged. The room has white walls, a large window with natural light, and a single large plant. The mood is warm, connected, and genuinely human. No one is performing. Shot on medium format digital. 35mm lens. Natural window light. Shallow depth of field. Editorial photography. Scandinavian interior. Muted, warm tones.

Emotional objective: To show that Premium Care professionals are part of a community, not isolated workers
