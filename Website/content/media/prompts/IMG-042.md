Overhead shot of a small oak coffee table in a Danish living room. On the table: two ceramic mugs (one sage green, one dusty blue), a small plate with two Danish butter cookies, a leather-bound notebook, and a simple pen. A soft wool blanket is draped over the arm of a mid-century sofa, visible at the edge of the frame. Natural window light falls across the table from the left. The mood is warm, ritualistic, and quietly inviting. Shot on medium format digital. 50mm lens from above. Natural window light. Moderate depth of field. Editorial still life photography. Scandinavian minimalism. Muted, warm tones.
