Three PremiumCare professionals in natural linen uniforms sit in a bright staff room, having a discussion over coffee. They sit on comfortable Danish design chairs around a small oak table. The room has white walls, a large window with natural light, and a single bookshelf with care manuals. The mood is supportive, professional, and warm. Shot on medium format digital. 50mm lens. Natural window light. Shallow depth of field. Editorial photography. Scandinavian interior. Muted, warm tones.
